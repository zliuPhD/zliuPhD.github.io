Research

My research interests encompass the fields of control theory, stochastic systems, probability, optimization, and machine learning. Currently, my research is focused on but not limited to the safety, reachability and control syhthesis of stochastic control systems, as well as their applications.

  • Planning and Control for Stochastic Systems. Utilize advanced techniques in stochastic system control to facilitate uncertainty-aware planning and robust controller synthesis in stochastic environments.
  • Uncertainty-Aware Controller Synthesis for Dynamical Systems. Design computationally tractable controllers that provide formal, high-probability (e.g., 99.99%) safety guarantees for safety-critical dynamical systems.
  • Safe Control in Robotics. Develop scalable, safety-assured, and real-time implementable control policies for robotics.
  • Analysis of Stochastic Differential Equations (SDE). Leverage cutting-edge theories in applied mathematics to analyze the stability, reachability and robustness of stochastic systems modelled by SDE.
